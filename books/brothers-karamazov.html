The Brothers Karamazov by Fyodor Dostoevsky

Published: 1880

This is my favorite book because, this story articulated aspects of myself that I have never managed to articulate. It is a Russian murder mystery, but that is only the vehicle for the narrative. This book is about the characters. It is about reason, faith, and passion personified as three brothers with depravity for a father whom they both loathe and love in their own ways, the story follows their lives and gives flesh to the drama of struggling with faith in a fallen world.
