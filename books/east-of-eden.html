East of Eden by John Steinbeck

Published: 1952

A multi-generational family drama in early 20th-century California. Steinbeck weaves themes from Genesis into the lives of a vast array of characters to draw out the timeless struggle between good and evil in each of us.Normally I'm an advocate for the value of activley reading over passivly listening to a book, but in this case the narritive is well suited to spoken word.
