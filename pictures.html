Pictures

Welcome to my gallery. Almost all the photos below were taken with a Canon 7D on a fixed 40mm lens. I like the fixed lens because you cannot rely on zoom to frame your photos. You need to move your body and interact with the subject, which makes it feel more like I am working with the camera instead of just operating it. I also love the texture and look of photos taken by this particular camera. It was a top-of-the-line camera 15 years ago, so the photos are high definition enough to be detailed, with enough noise from the camera to give them a nostalgic feeling. For me, the photos remind me of surfing or snowboarding magazines I used to read growing up. I always shoot in manual to force myself to learn and understand the camera. It took a lot of overexposed and ruined shots to get a few good ones at first, but after thousands of pictures a few were destined to be interesting. I have shared some of them below. You will find categories of photos I have taken below. Apologies for the final two categories. I have not finished debugging the website..
